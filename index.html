Christian Futurism: Principles & Arguments

"God bless thee, my son; I will give thee the greatest jewel I have.... The end of our foundation is the knowledge of causes, and secret motions of things; and the enlarging of the bounds of human empire, to the effecting of all things possible"

— Sir. Francis Bacon, The New Atlantis

We envision a future where humanity's development aligns with God's cosmic purpose. Rooted in Christian eschatology and inspired by early Christian thinkers, Christian futurism is not an adaptation of secular futurism but a movement that subordinates futuristic visions to Christianity by upholding humanity's mandate to cultivate the cosmos, harness technology for God's glory, and direct all aspects of life toward Christ. We espouse an active role for the church in God's unfolding plan and expect that history's progression will affirm the truth of Christianity as humanity moves toward unity in Christ and the completion of God's Kingdom.
